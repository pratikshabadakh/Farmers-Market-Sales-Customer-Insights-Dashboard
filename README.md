#  Farmers Market Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard

## 🔹 Short Description

The **Farmers Market Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI solution built to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, vendor contribution, and product trends in a farmers market environment.
It enables stakeholders to quickly identify revenue drivers, high-value customers, and top-performing products for data-driven decision making.



##  Tech Stack

* **Power BI** – Dashboard development & data visualization
* **SQL** – Data extraction and transformation
* **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
* **DAX** – KPI measures and calculated metrics

---

## 📂 Data Source

Public/sample farmers market retail dataset used for analytical and learning purposes.
The dataset includes information about customers, vendors, products, sales transactions, and quantities.



##  Key Features

* ✅ Interactive KPI cards (Total Sales, Customers, Vendors, Booths)
* ✅ Product-wise sales distribution analysis
* ✅ Vendor performance comparison
* ✅ Customer-level sales insights
* ✅ Dynamic filtering and drill-down capability
* ✅ Clean, business-ready dashboard layout
* ✅ End-to-end data analysis workflow (data → model → visualization)



## ⭐ Dashboard Highlights

* 📈 **$8.67K Total Sales** analyzed across the market
* 👥 **29 Unique Customers** identified from **1003 total records**
* 🏪 **9 Vendors** and **7 Booths** performance tracked
* 🥇 **Top products** contribute the majority of revenue
* 🎯 Customer-level analysis highlights high-value buyers
* 📊 Vendor concentration shows sales dependency patterns



##  Business Impact

* **Revenue visibility:** Enables quick identification of top revenue contributors (products and vendors).
* **Customer intelligence:** Helps business teams recognize high-value customers for targeted marketing.
* **Vendor performance monitoring:** Supports fair vendor evaluation and booth allocation decisions.
* **Inventory planning:** Product demand insights assist in better stock and supply planning.
* **Executive reporting:** KPI cards provide at-a-glance performance monitoring.



## 🔍 Key Insights

* 🥇 A few products contribute a **disproportionately high share of sales**, indicating Pareto behavior.
* 👤 Certain customers (e.g., top buyers) drive significantly higher revenue than others.
* 🏪 Sales are **highly concentrated with a single vendor**, suggesting dependency risk.
* 📦 Product category performance varies widely, highlighting optimization opportunities.
* 📉 Some customers show low purchase frequency — potential for targeted campaigns.



## 🎯 Recommended Actions

* 🚀 Focus promotions on top-performing products to maximize revenue.
* 🎯 Implement loyalty or retention programs for high-value customers.
* ⚖️ Diversify vendor contribution to reduce over-dependency risk.
* 📦 Optimize inventory based on product demand patterns.
* 📣 Target low-frequency customers with personalized offers.
